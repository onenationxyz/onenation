The Manifest

#ONENATION

Whereas

  • Earth is one massive living organism.
  • An infinity of life forms coexist interdependently.
  • Humanity has a major influence over other terrestrial realms.
  • Each human being is a unique and individualized cell of the planet.
  • Resilience of an organism is maintained when its cells are fully free to play their roles at the service of the Whole and its parts.

I proclaim

  • My gratitude towards Life that animates all things.
  • My deep respect for all inhabitants of our earthly vessel.
  • The primacy of the biological and conscious being that I am, over the legal entity created after my birth.
  • The legitimacy of my creative power as long as it excludes any form of theft, deception, moral or physical violence.
  • The absolute responsibility for all my thoughts, words and actions of my own free-will.

I undertake

  • To process my fears so that, with love, I take care of myself and every form of life.
  • To seize my power as a living Human Being and use it to serve harmony.
  • To resort to joyful and pacific disobedience whenever fundamental rights of Life are in danger.

I, _______________________________________ , acknowledge this pact that links me to myself and the Earth. Today ________________________ , I connect through my heart with the emancipated Beings of One Nation across all oceans and continents.

¿FAQ?

  • WHAT - What is One Nation? A new country?

    One Nation is neither a new country nor a new world order.

    It isn't an association, a syndicate, a political party, a NGO, a lobby, a company nor anything similar to a legal institution.
    Do we have to mention it: One Nation is not a sect.

    So, what is it ?

    There is an emancipation wave at the planetary scale silently growing, days after days, months after months and for decades.
    We had the idea to give it a name and chose to call it One Nation...

    #ONENATION

    We could see One Nation as a label or a hashtag to recognise each others human beings ready to act for harmony and common good. One Nation is composed of Avatars (Unchained human beings) living or not living in Oasis (Unchained lands)

    This isn't literally an organisation. Its operation is natural, organic, based on stigmergy.

  • WHY - Why call it « One Nation »?
    One

    Whether you Like it or not, we all live on the same planet.
    Earth is the link between all of us.

    Instead of wreaking the spacial ship we all share, we took an oath of living and spreading harmony together, inside us and around us.

    Nation

    We keep away from the political aspect of a nation, and any ideological believes.

    We do hear the word "nation in the sense of « A vast group of Human Beings aware of its unity and its common wish to live together ».

  • STIGMERGY - How One Nation is organized?

    In a word : stigmery (See on Wikipédia)

    A "non-organisation"

    One Nation is a « planetary non-organisation » decentralized, without governance nor borders…
    It fully embraces stigmergy: this special way of taking decision in a spontaneous and decentralized way, with no chiefs nor central organisation. Each individual takes its own initiatives without waiting for approbation nor authorisation of any other being or entity.

    Today in most collective organisation, we can see rules everywhere, circulars, comities... We prefer take the bet of spontaneousness, of sharing, of sense of responsability and trust that each one of us acts for the common good.

    Once we got ridden of never-ending meetings, looking for a consensus, we are free of acting accordingly to our natural momentum. We believe in the balance of all initiatives. What is painfull to do for someone if often source of enthousiasm for another.

    How does it work ?

    Stigmergy is the art of living and let live, organise daring the let-it-be and trust. I let go my will to control everything and I trust in other's desires, because I know he/she is doing his/her best to decide, think, act in order to make the collective lifestyle better for all. I remember he/she is trusting me as well. This trust is guaranteed by the fact he/she also sign the manifest.

    From a need, many awnsers can be intended. Instead of searching for THE best solution, why not trying them all ?

    Each and every one creates, invents, tries, experiments on his/her side. The most efficient solution will be naturally retained, used and improved. And the less obvious will be forgotten, without any need to consult each others. Solutions evolve through time with us, embracing an organic and simple way...

    Relearn

    We learned to receive instructions, homeworks, since young in school.
    We need to relearn for this world to act spontaneously, an autonomous way, to validated ourselves our intuitions and to follow them. Once this trust in spontneous action retrived, we shall then be invincible because elusive.

    More plentifull and connected we will be, more this parallel decentralized network will be strong and resilient to offer a real alternative.

  • TELEGRAM - How to contribute? Meet? Get in touch?

    There is no "official" One Nation.
    Each one is free to be a new starting point to incarnate One Nation on earth.

    Telegram

    A first momentum is growing on Telegram
    Telegram's conception and philosophy matches pretty well with One Nation's operation (see [stigmergie]#faq-stigmergie). It gathers 500+ milions active users concerned by the privacy and respect of theirs data.

    In a fast glance

    Telegram is a messaging application working on computer (Linux, Mac OS, Windows) and on smartphone (Android et iOS).

    Step #1 Download Telegram : https://telegram.org/

    Step #2 Join the welcome thread : https://t.me/onenation_en_welcome

    Design

    Everyone is free to create a group and organize discutions.
    There is no central group and no permission to ask before to start. Groups that are very actives will diversify naturally in sub-groups. Useless groups will probably not get any visitors.

    Telegram offers a search function to get a list of all public groups of One Nation
    Here is a glossary for groups name so we can find them more easily

    Prefixes

    Each Englsih-speaking thread begins by onenation_en_
    Each French-speaking thread begins by onenation_fr_
    etc etc …

    NB : Philosophically, we are looking to separate groups by « locale » instead of « countries ».

    Groups examples

    onenation_en_avatars : presentation of english-speaking avatars
    onenation_fr_avatars : presentation of french-speaking avatars
    …

    You can also create private groups if you feel like sharing in restricted circles.
    It's also possible to create temporary groups not devoted to persist.

    Group rules

    Each group provides a README pinned message for everyone to know the rules in this group.

    For exemple in the group onenation_en_avatars, each one present him/herself with no discussion. If you want to discuss about it, you can contact individuals you may send a personnal message.

    Create a group
    1. Open Telegram
    2. Create a group
    3. Invite a first member (mandatory)
    4. Group name : onenation_en_groupname (see higher)
    5. not over! for the moment it's a private group...
    6. Open group parameters then "Edit"
    7. Change group type
    • pick « Public »
    • Link : t.me/onenation_en_groupname (same name)
    1. Add a picture (the logo for example)
    2. Add a description
    3. Write the first message README with game rules for the group
    4. Pin this message so that everyone can find it easily.
    5. Announce this new group somewhere else where it's appropriated.
    To conclude

    If other groups organize themself somewhere else than Telegram, it's perfect !
    The most important is the fact that something is happening 😉

  • EMANCIPATION - From whom does One Nation emancipates itself?
    In one sentence

    One Nation emancipate from any authority illegitimate.

    Parabol of the emancipation

    Imagine this :
    You live at your parents's home, your individuality is fully built and you are now able to choose by yourself.

    At your parents's house, there are very important rule like for example :

    • Everyone eats at table, together, at fixed hour.
    • Cat isn't allowed to go in bedrooms.

    Sometimes you listen to your needs/wills. You make your own meal alone in the middle of the afternoon. Or you open the door to the cat so that he can spend the night with you... Acting this way, you put yourself in a disobedience position.

    One day you will have enough of the feeling of being an outlaw where you live while obviously, you don't bother anybody. This day, you decide, with no anger, to look for your own place. This place where you can eat pastas at 4pm and sleep alongside your cat without breaking any law.

    For One Nation, it's the same thing!

    It is impossible to keep our integrity and our creativity in this world full of 💩️🤕🤦‍ rules. We do not expect anything from government and others institutions who, hundred times proved their irresponsibility and bad faith.

    So bye bye ! From now, we answer with a happy and calm "no" to all illegitimate injonctions.

The 🥱 questions

  • UN - Do you intend to get international recognition?

    One Nation dosn't need recognition from any legal entity in place. Especially not with United Nations which represents every countries playing on the same board game.

    We do not ask permission, we take possession of our natural et legitimate right to live peacefully on this earth in harmony with every living beings. Since countries have been inable to offer us this right, let's take it by ourselves

    Finally, the only recognition that matters is the one we give ourselves and to each others.

  • CONSTITUTION - Will One Nation provide itself with its own law texts?

    Never 😉
    We shall not build the new with old ingredients.
    Our only law is: trust, kindness and respect.
    Those are the necessary values for a peaceful cohabitation on earth. They are written in the manifest.

    One Nation will never promulgate any text of law.

    One nation is not philosophically (or technically) able to write and apply law texts. For a law to exists, we do need an institution in charge of writting texts, and many others to apply them (usually by force). It's the opposite of One Nation's spirit. We believe no law is valid and applicable on all parts of the planetat the same time.

    Of course, locally, it is up to each Oasis, each human being to edict and apply his/her own life rules.
    We imagine One Nation as a catalizer of creative solution in order to facilitate relation between human beings. Each solution is created with the idea that a good design can prevent us from many text of laws. This open solutions have no mandatory trait.
